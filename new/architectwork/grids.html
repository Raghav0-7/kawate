Grids

The proposed design for the Charles Correa Memorial in Goa encapsulates the essence of the renowned architect’s principles and philosophy. It features a dynamic arrangement of pavilions in a spiral formation, symbolizing the cyclical nature of architecture’s relationship with culture and nature, while drawing inspiration from traditional Goan architectural elements and sustainable design practices. The memorial offers a serene oasis within bustling Goa, incorporating lush gardens and water features, fostering a sense of tranquility.
