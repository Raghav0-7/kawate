Working Drawing

The working drawing design for the two-story clubhouse project with a basement is a meticulously planned architectural marvel. The basement serves as a functional space for storage, utilities, and recreational activities, featuring proper ventilation and lighting. The ground floor incorporates a grand entrance, lounge areas, and recreational spaces, all bathed in natural light with large windows and a seamless connection to outdoor amenities. The upper floor is dedicated to private gathering spaces, including meeting rooms and a spacious terrace for social events.
