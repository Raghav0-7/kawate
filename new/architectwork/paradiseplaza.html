Paradise Plaza

The design of the pavilion in the heart of Ahmednagar embodies the philosophy of usable space, creating a multifaceted community hub that seamlessly integrates a neighborhood market with sports facilities, a library, an amphitheater, a skatepark, a yoga center, and more, all thoughtfully arranged within lush green surroundings. Emphasizing accessibility, sustainability, and a harmonious blend of modern and traditional aesthetics, the pavilion aims to enrich the lives of residents by providing a dynamic and inclusive environment that fosters social interaction, physical well-being and cultural engagement.
