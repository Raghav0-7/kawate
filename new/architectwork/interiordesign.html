Interior Design

In this 3BHK flat designed for a family of five, a modern theme unfolds with a personalized touch. The retired father's space exudes sophistication with warm neutrals, while his wife's domain radiates comfort with soft pastels. The son's area embraces a vibrant palette, reflecting his dynamic personality, while the daughter's space incorporates calming blues. The daughter-in-law's corner balances elegance and modernity with muted tones. Together, the interior design weaves a cohesive narrative, blending individual tastes into a harmonious and stylish family haven."
